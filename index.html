Crystal Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crytal you will add a specific number amount to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the random number that was generated at the beginning of the game. The value of each crytal is hidden from you until you click on it. Everytime the game starts, a new random number will be given to you. Good luck!
